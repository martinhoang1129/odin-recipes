Bo Luc Lac

Description

This is a Vietnamese dish. The dish uses skirt steak, new york steak, or ribeye. The meat is marinaded with soy sauce, oyster sauce, sugar, honey, salt, pepper, sesame oil, and garlic. The recipe sides/decorators are tomatoes, lettuce, and onions. This simple recipe takes 15 minutes to make.

Ingredients

  • steak meat
  • soy sauce
  • oyster sauce
  • sesame oil
  • sugar
  • honey
  • salt & pepper
  • yellow onion
  • lettuce
  • tomatoes
    •

    Steps

  • Dice steak meat and marinade with soy sauce, garlic, oyster sauce, sesame oil, sugar, honey, salt, pepper
  • Slice yellow onion, lettuce, tomatoes
  • Heat up pan and sautee steak meat for 5 minutes
  • In the last 2 minutes, add yellow onion and sautee too
  • On a plate, lay out the tomatoes and lettuce. Pour the cooked Bo Luc Lac meat onto the plate and serve!
