Pho

Description

This is a chicken pho recipe, also known as Pho Ga. This dish stems from Vietnamese cruisine, and it is now one of the most popular noodle soup dishes in the world. The recipe consist of rice noodle, chicken broth, cilantro, chicken, salt, pepper, bean spouts, ginger, cinnamon, fish sauce, garlic, hoisen sauce, and siracha sauce.

Ingredients

  • rice noodle
  • chicken broth
  • chicken
  • Siracha sauce
  • Hoisen Sauce
  • ginger
  • onion
  • coriander
  • star anise
  • cilantro
  • green onion
    •

    Steps

  • Boil water, chicken, ginger, cinnamon, onion, coriander, star anise inside for 30 minutes to make the broth
  • Remove the chicken
  • shread all chicken in a seperate bowl
  • boil rice noodle and rinse in cold water
  • Combine rice noodle, shreadded chicken, broth, salt, pepper, cilantro, green onion. Add sauce to liking and serve!
